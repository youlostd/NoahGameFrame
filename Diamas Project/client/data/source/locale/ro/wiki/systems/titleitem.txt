[CENTER_ALIGN]Title system

With the item "Tincture of Titles" you can create your own title, which then appears 
above your name or guild name. The following applies:

- Alphanumeric characters (a-Z, 0-9) allowed
- 16.581.375 colors selectable via the RGB generator
- Preview of the selected title
- Overwritable with the same item
- Removable with "Tincture of titles (removal)".

Not allowed are special characters or offensive / insulting expressions.
